Coco Peat

Our 100% natural coco peat is a multi-purpose growing medium. Known for its exceptional water retention and superior aeration properties, it acts as an ideal soil conditioner for nurseries, greenhouses, and extensive hydroponic cultivations.

Our Coco Peat blocks are produced from natural coconut husk pith. Thoroughly washed and dried to maintain low electrical conductivity (EC) and optimal pH levels, they provide an ideal environment for root development and healthy plant growth.

Compressed at a 5:1 ratio, these blocks expand easily upon adding water, making them highly cost-effective and convenient for commercial agriculture, floriculture, home gardening, and soil conditioning.
