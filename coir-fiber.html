Coir Fiber

Our golden coir fiber is extracted from 100% natural coconut husks, thoroughly cleaned, and processed under strict quality guidelines to ensure durability, high tensile strength, and natural resistance to saltwater. The final product is hydraulically compressed into dense bales for secure, space-efficient transport.

As a versatile and eco-friendly raw material, Coir Fiber Bales are widely utilized in automobile upholstery (such as car seats and dashboards), geo-textiles, coir spring mattresses, furniture padding, erosion control blankets, drainage filtering systems, ropes, twines, mats, and brushes.
