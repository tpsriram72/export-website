About Our Company

Delivering high-quality Coir Fiber and Coco Peat through strict quality control and global logistics execution.

Welcome to Top Selection Export

Based in Andhra Pradesh, India, TOP SELECTION EXPORT PRIVATE LIMITED is a dedicated merchant exporter bringing high-grade, sustainable agricultural products to the global market.

We act as a vital bridge between India's rich agricultural resources and international buyers who require strict quality compliance. By partnering with certified local manufacturers, we ensure that every batch of coir fiber and coco peat is processed to meet international standards before dispatch.

Our mission is to support global horticulture, hydroponics, and industrial applications with 100% natural, eco-friendly, and biodegradable solutions. We take complete ownership of the trade lifecycle—from rigorous quality inspections and standardized packaging to customs documentation and container logistics.

  • ✓ Direct Sourcing from Coconut Sourcing Belts
  • ✓ Double-Tested Quality Standards (EC, pH, Moisture)
  • ✓ Flexible Packaging (Bales, Blocks, Custom Loadings)
  • ✓ Multi-Port Export Capability (Tuticorin, Chennai, Vizag)
🌱 100% Eco-Friendly

🌟 Our Vision

To establish India's high-quality coir and coco peat products as a global standard for sustainable growth, built on the foundations of supreme integrity, transparency, and client satisfaction.
